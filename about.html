About Me

Hello, I’m Meti, a Software Engineering student with a growing passion for web development. My guiding principle is setting the standard for excellence, and I live by the belief that working today makes tomorrow better. I am at the beginning of my professional journey, but I approach every challenge with responsibility, loyalty, and a commitment to continuous learning. Right now, I’m building my foundation in programming with Python, Java, and Git, while also sharpening essential soft skills such as communication, adaptability, critical thinking, networking, and lifelong learning. What excites me most about technology is its power to solve problems and create opportunities. Even though I’m still learning, I see every project — no matter how small — as a chance to grow and contribute. My current focus is on web development, where I’m exploring how ideas can be transformed into interactive, user-friendly experiences. Beyond academics, I enjoy watching movies, hanging out with friends, exploring art, and participating in fellowship activities. These hobbies keep me balanced, creative, and connected to people — qualities I believe are just as important in building great software. Looking ahead, my goal is to become a full-stack web developer, combining front-end creativity with back-end problem-solving. I also have a strong interest in cybersecurity, where I want to contribute to making systems safer, and in data structures and algorithms (DSA), which I see as the backbone of efficient and powerful software. By blending these areas, I aim to grow into a versatile developer who can design, secure, and optimize solutions that truly make a difference. This portfolio is a reflection of my journey — a place where I share my progress, showcase my skills, and invite others to connect. I may be at the start, but I’m determined to keep moving forward, step by step, toward excellence.
